About CryptMarbel

CryptMarbel is a modern, secure, and user-friendly macOS application designed to protect your sensitive files and directories using state-of-the-art public-key cryptography. Built with native Apple technologies, CryptMarbel provides military-grade encryption with an intuitive interface that anyone can use.

Whether you need to securely share confidential documents, protect personal files, or ensure your data remains private, CryptMarbel makes encryption accessible without compromising on security. Your private keys are safely stored in the macOS Keychain, ensuring maximum protection.

How It Works

CryptMarbel uses a hybrid encryption approach that combines the best of both worlds:

  1. Key Generation: On first launch, a secure Curve25519 key pair is generated and stored in your Keychain
  2. Encryption: Files are encrypted with AES-256-GCM, and the encryption key is protected using the recipient's public key
  3. Secure Sharing: Share your public key freely - only your private key can decrypt files sent to you
  4. Decryption: Use your private key to decrypt files, restoring them to their original state

Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 1, 2025

1. Introduction

CryptMarbel ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how CryptMarbel handles your data and ensures your security.

2. Data Collection

CryptMarbel does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The app operates entirely locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Private Keys: Securely stored in the macOS Keychain with hardware-level encryption
  • Public Keys: Stored in app preferences for easy access and sharing
  • Encrypted Files: Saved to locations you choose on your local filesystem
  • Metadata: Encryption metadata (nonces, tags, file names) stored alongside encrypted files

All data remains on your device and under your control. We have no access to your keys, files, or any other information.

4. Data Security

CryptMarbel implements industry-standard security measures:

  • AES-256-GCM: Military-grade symmetric encryption for file contents
  • Curve25519: Modern elliptic curve cryptography for key exchange
  • macOS Keychain: Hardware-backed secure storage for private keys
  • No Network Access: The app does not connect to the internet or transmit any data

5. Third-Party Services

CryptMarbel does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools. The app is completely self-contained and operates offline.

6. User Responsibilities

As a user of CryptMarbel, you are responsible for:

  • Keeping your private key secure (stored in Keychain)
  • Backing up important unencrypted files
  • Safely sharing your public key with intended recipients
  • Never sharing your private key with anyone
  • Keeping metadata files (.metadata.json) alongside encrypted files

7. Data Loss

Important: If you lose your private key (e.g., by resetting your Keychain or reinstalling macOS without backup), you will permanently lose access to files encrypted for you. We cannot recover lost keys or decrypt files without the private key.

8. Open Source

CryptMarbel is open source software. You can review the complete source code on GitHub to verify our privacy and security claims.

9. Children's Privacy

CryptMarbel does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app can be used by anyone who can operate macOS.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this website with an updated "Last updated" date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or CryptMarbel's privacy practices, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

12. Your Rights

Since CryptMarbel does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over all data generated by the app, as it resides entirely on your device.

13. Disclaimer

CryptMarbel is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind. While we strive to provide secure encryption, users should implement additional security measures for critical data and maintain proper backups.
